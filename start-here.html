Tell us what's not happening.

BEAM works backwards from a stalled behavior. Pick the situation that most closely describes your problem and we'll route you to the right place.

What's the behavior that isn't happening?

Situation 01

People visit but don't convert.

Traffic is there. The offer is clear. But visitors are not submitting forms, booking calls, completing purchases, or taking the next step.

Diagnose the conversion block →
Situation 02

We keep rewriting copy but nothing changes.

The message has been tested and revised. The creative has been refreshed. The behavior still does not change. Something else is in the way.

Find what messaging can't fix →
Situation 03

People seem interested but keep delaying.

Engagement is high. People open emails, visit pages, and express interest. But they consistently defer the action. Something about the moment is not right.

See an emotional state diagnosis →
Situation 04

The environment might be the problem.

The offer is strong and the message is clear but the physical or digital conditions surrounding the decision are working against you.
